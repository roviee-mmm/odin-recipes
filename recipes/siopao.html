Bola-Bola Siopao

Back to Homepage

This recipe is courtesy of Katherin Jao of Yummy ph

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups pork use ground pork
  • 1/2 Cup fresh shiitake mushrooms chopped
  • 2 tablespoons Soy Sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 piece Egg beaten
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • chicken powder to taste
  • 4 cups all-purpose flour plus 1 cup for dusting
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon Salt
  • 2 teaspoons Instant yeast
  • 1/2 Cup sugar
  • 1 1/4 Cup water
  • 2 tablespoons Shortening

Preparation

Step 1: Make the bola-bola filling
Mix together ground pork, shiitake mushroom, light soy sauce, sesame oil, sugar, egg, cornstarch in a bowl.
Season to taste. Set aside in the chiller until ready to use.
Step 2: Make the dough
Whisk together all-purpose flour, baking powder, salt, instant yeast, and sugar in a bowl.
Make a well in the center and pour in lukewarm water. Add shortening.
Mix with a wooden spoon until the dough comes together, but is not yet smooth (this is also known as the shaggy dough stage).
Step 3: Prepare the dough
Remove dough from bowl and transfer to a clean work surface dusted with 1 cup flour.
Knead the dough until smooth and elastic, about 12 to 15 minutes.
Transfer dough to a lightly greased bowl cover with a damp piece of cloth and let rest for 30 to 40 minutes or until double in size.
Step 4: Form the siopaos
Divide dough into 16 pieces and form into bowls.
Using your palm, flatten each into a 5-inch circle. Fill each dough circle with a spoonful of bola-bola filling.
Seal by forming into a ball. Repeat with the rest of the ingredients.
Make pleats on top of the buns, if desired.
Step 5: Prepare to cook the siopaos
Place buns in a steamer lined with parchment paper, leaving spaces in between each bun.
Let buns rise, covered with a thin piece of cloth, for 30 to 40 minutes or until double in size.
Step 6: Cooking the siopaos
Steam over high heat for 18 to 20 minutes or until puffed and cooked.
